Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope, but most brand identity projects deliver in 4-6 weeks and web platforms in 8-12 weeks from kickoff to launch.

What does the discovery phase include?

We conduct stakeholder interviews, competitor analysis, audience research, and brand audits to build a strategic foundation before any design work begins.

Can we work together on a retainer basis?

Yes. Many clients choose our Enterprise retainer model for ongoing design and development support, with dedicated bandwidth and priority turnaround.
